About Us

At Aqua Splash, we invite you to dive into a world of exhilarating aquatic adventures and unforgettable moments.
Nestled in the heart of Dagupan City, Pangasinan Aqua Splash stands as the premier destination for families, thrill-seekers, and water lovers of all ages.
Founded in 2024 Aqua Splash was born out of a vision to create a haven where fun meets relaxation, and where memories are made to last a lifetime.

Attractions

Welcome to Aqua Splash, where we pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of aquatic attractions and amenities designed to cater to the enjoyment of our valued guests.
Within our expansive facility, guests are invited to partake in a plethora of aquatic delights, including an assortment of exhilarating water slides guaranteed to thrill even the most adventurous of spirits. Whether careening down twisting chutes, spiraling through exhilarating tunnels, or plunging into refreshing pools, our array of slides promises an adrenaline-fueled experience like no other.
Furthermore, guests can indulge in the tranquil ambiance of our pristine pools, meticulously maintained to provide a serene environment for relaxation and enjoyment. From our gently lapping wave pool, reminiscent of oceanic shores, to our invitingly serene lazy river, there's a water-based escape to suit every preference.
Additionally, for those seeking to commemorate their unforgettable Aqua Splash experience, our onsite souvenir shop offers a curated selection of mementos and memorabilia. From branded merchandise to personalized keepsakes, our shop provides the perfect opportunity to take home a tangible reminder of your aquatic adventures.
At Aqua Splash, we prioritize the safety and satisfaction of our guests above all else. Our dedicated team of lifeguards undergo rigorous training to ensure the highest standards of safety are upheld throughout the park. Furthermore, our commitment to cleanliness and hygiene ensures that guests can enjoy a pristine environment in which to create lasting memories.
Whether you're seeking thrills, relaxation, or simply a memorable day out with loved ones, Aqua Splash offers an unparalleled aquatic experience that promises fun and excitement for all. We cordially invite you to join us and embark on a journey of aquatic discovery unlike any other.
